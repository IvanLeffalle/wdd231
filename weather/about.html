This is an example of a Weather App for the WDD 231 course.

About

Welcome to the Weather App! This application was developed as part of the WDD 231 course to provide real-time weather updates based on your location. It features a clean and intuitive design that allows you to easily check the current weather, forecast data, and more. The app pulls weather data from a reliable API, delivering accurate temperature, humidity, wind speed, and other essential weather information. Whether you're planning your day or need to know if you should bring an umbrella, this app will keep you informed.
